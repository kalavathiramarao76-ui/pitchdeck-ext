Detailed Description

DeckForge is an AI-powered pitch deck generator that helps founders create investor-ready materials in minutes. From full 10-slide decks to concise investor emails, DeckForge has everything you need to fundraise with confidence.


Full 10-Slide Deck: Generate a complete pitch deck covering Problem, Solution, Market Size, Business Model, Traction, GTM Strategy, Competition, Team, Financials, and The Ask.


Individual Slide Writer: Need just one section? Generate any slide with deep, focused content.


Investor Email: Concise, compelling outreach emails under 200 words, ready to send to investors.


Pitch Script: 5-minute pitch script with section-by-section timing guides for practice.


Competition Matrix: Analyze 4+ competitors with feature comparison tables showing your differentiation.


Data Integration: Pull startup data directly from Crunchbase and AngelList pages for accurate competitor and market analysis.
